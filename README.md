**Platform Engineer | Infrastructure Architect**

> *Infrastructure should empower developers, not block them.*

I am a Platform Engineer with over 8 years of experience designing, scaling, and securing enterprise-grade infrastructure. Currently operating within a Center of Excellence (CoE) at Tata Consultancy Services (TCS), I specialize in building next-generation Internal Developer Platforms (IDPs). My work focuses on bridging the gap between complex Kubernetes infrastructure and seamless developer workflows by combining rigorous system architecture with a strong emphasis on Developer Experience (DevEx).

---

### Professional Focus

* **Infrastructure & IDP Architecture:** Designing and scaling multi-tenant Kubernetes clusters. Building resilient Internal Developer Platforms that reduce cognitive load and accelerate deployment lifecycles.
* **Systems Engineering & Automation:** Developing custom Go controllers and robust internal tooling to automate infrastructure operations.
* **Security & Access Management:** Architecting scalable RBAC models and enforcing enterprise-grade security standards across cloud-native environments.
* **Tooling Usability:** Advocating for unified design systems within internal tooling to ensure functional, accessible, and intuitive developer workflows.

### Engineering Leadership & Philosophy

Beyond system architecture, I am actively committed to advancing engineering culture and practices:

* **Mentorship:** Guiding engineers through the technical and paradigm shifts required to transition from traditional IT to modern platform engineering.
* **Workflow Optimization:** Exploring the intersection of design and code to optimize developer environments and streamline terminal-based workflows.
